# ১. Business Strategy Consultant Mode

## 🧠 Scenario

রাফি একটি নতুন EdTech startup শুরু করতে চায়।
কিন্তু market crowded। কোথা থেকে শুরু করবে বুঝতে পারছে না।

সে Claude-কে market analyst বানালো।

## ✅ Easy but Powerful Prompt

```text id="y2n4kp"
Act like an experienced business consultant.

Analyze the [industry name] market.

Help me understand:
- Main competitors
- Market opportunities
- Biggest risks
- Customer pain points
- Possible growth strategies

Use:
- Porter’s Five Forces
- Simple competitor comparison
- Practical business recommendations

Keep the explanation professional but easy to understand.
```

## 🎯 Result

Claude:

* Market breakdown দিবে
* Competitor analysis করবে
* Weakness + opportunity বের করবে
* Action plan suggest করবে

# ৫. Personal AI Tutor Mode (Feynman Technique)

## 🧠 Scenario

নাবিল Physics এর একটি topic বুঝতে পারছে না।

সে Claude-কে tutor বানালো।

## ✅ Easy but Workable Prompt

```text id="5xh1ew"
Teach me [topic] using the Feynman Technique.

Requirements:
- Explain like a beginner
- Avoid difficult jargon
- Use simple real-life examples
- Divide into small modules
- Ask me questions after each section

Do not move forward until I understand.
```

## 🎯 Result

Claude:

* Topic সহজ করবে
* Practice question দিবে
* Learning যাচাই করবে
